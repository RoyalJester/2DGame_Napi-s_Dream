2D Game.
OBJECTIVE: 

Keep the Sheep moving down using the arrow keys!
Aliens are coming, and they wont leave until they capture our beloved Napi.
Aliens are using their advanced next gen technology to pull the ground up!
And Napi our sheep is running crazy all over the place trying to save its life!
